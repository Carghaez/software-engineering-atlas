Raft (leader election + log replication):

election (per term):
    a follower whose timeout fires becomes a candidate, term += 1
    it votes for itself and asks every peer for a vote
    a node grants AT MOST ONE vote per term (first-come)
    candidate with a MAJORITY of votes -> leader for that term
    # any two majorities overlap, so a term has at most one leader (safety)

replication (leader):
    on client command: append {term, command} to local log
    send AppendEntries to followers; they append if their log matches
    once a MAJORITY have stored an entry, it is COMMITTED
    leader advances commitIndex; followers apply committed entries in order

failure handling:
    heartbeats reset follower timeouts; a silent leader triggers a new election
    higher term seen -> step down to follower
