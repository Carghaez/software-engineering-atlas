Binary Space Partitioning (recursively split space by hyperplanes):

build(polygons):
    pick one polygon's plane as the splitter
    front = polygons wholly in front; back = polygons wholly behind
    (polygons straddling the plane are SPLIT in two)
    return Node(plane, build(front), build(back))

# Signature use: exact back-to-front (painter's) ordering from any viewpoint,
# with no per-frame sorting:
paint(node, viewer):
    if viewer is in FRONT of node.plane:
        paint(node.back);  draw(node.poly);  paint(node.front)
    else:
        paint(node.front); draw(node.poly);  paint(node.back)

# Visiting the far side first guarantees nearer surfaces overdraw farther ones.
